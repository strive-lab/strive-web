The process, A to Z

Registration runs through one integrated MCA form — SPICe+ — but six things have to happen in roughly this order before it can be filed.

01

Name & Trademark Check

Confirm the name is free on MCA and unregistered on IP India.

02

Registered Office

Decide the address; gather a fresh utility bill, agreement, and NOC.

03

Digital Signatures

Each director obtains a Class 3 DSC via Aadhaar e-KYC.

04

Drafting

MOA, AOA, DIR-2, and subscriber declarations are prepared.

05

Professional Filing

A CA/CS certifies and files SPICe+ on the MCA portal.

06

Certificate Issued

Incorporation Certificate, PAN, TAN, and GSTIN arrive together.

Reference

Getting the one thing you don't already have

Everything above is either already in hand or drafted for you — except the Digital Signature Certificate, which has to be newly obtained per director.

1Choose a Certifying Authority

eMudhra, Sify, or NSDL are MCA-authorised providers. Any one works — pricing and turnaround are similar across all three.

2Complete Aadhaar e-KYC

Done entirely online: a webcam photo plus an Aadhaar OTP. No physical paperwork or in-person visit required.

3Receive the DSC

Typically issued within a few hours of successful verification. ₹800 – ₹2,000 per director

4Repeat for each director

All three founders need their own DSC — it can't be shared, and each is tied to that individual's Aadhaar and PAN.
