### 1. Founders' Agreement

**Between:** Yash, Dhruv, and Nirali
**Signed by:** All three founders
**When it applies:** Immediately upon signature — binding even though the company isn't incorporated yet

**What it covers:**
- Equity: Yash 55% / Dhruv 20% / Nirali 10% / 15% ESOP pool (unallocated)
- Vesting: Yash front-loaded (25% immediate + 24-month monthly vest, no cliff); Dhruv and Nirali on standard 4-year vesting with a 1-year cliff
- Domain-based decision authority (each founder owns their area, subject to Yash's approval), with unanimous consent required for major decisions (funding, new co-founders, equity changes, dissolution)
- IP assignment — all work product belongs to the company, not individuals
- Confidentiality, Good Leaver / Bad Leaver provisions, dispute resolution (Surat arbitration)
- **Governs the company as a whole** — not tied to any single product, and explicitly survives any future company name change
- **Effective Date is dynamic** — it's whatever date the last founder actually signs, not a fixed date
- Explicitly acknowledges the company is **not yet incorporated** and states this Agreement is what protects the three of you during that gap

**Action needed:** Get this physically signed and dated by all three of you soon — it has no effect until then.

**Public-facing document** — no signature needed, published on the website/app
**Applies to:** Every Student, TPO, Faculty, and HR user of Axis Strive

### 4. Pilot Agreement (Reusable Template)

**Between:** Strive Pulse and any partner institution
**Signed by:** Yash (or authorized founder) + the institution's authorized signatory
**Use this:** Every time you onboard a new college for a free pilot (RRU or any future institution)

**What it covers:** No-cost pilot terms, scope (what features are included), 15-day termination notice, non-exclusivity (you can pilot elsewhere, they can use other tools), and — importantly — **feedback/reference rights**, letting you use the pilot as a case study or reference later, with the institution's sign-off on wording.

**How to reuse:** Fill in the Institution Details table at the top (name, location, TPO contact, dates) — the rest of the document references that table automatically. Duplicate a fresh copy per institution; never edit an already-signed copy.

### 5. Mutual NDA (Reusable Template)

**Between:** Strive Pulse and any counterparty
**Signed by:** Yash + the other party
**Use this:** Before sharing anything sensitive — product plans, metrics, pilot terms — with a new college, investor, or potential partner, ahead of a formal agreement.

**What it covers:** Standard mutual confidentiality — 2-year term, 3-year survival on each disclosure, standard exclusions (public info, independently developed, etc.), injunctive relief for breach.

**Applies to:** Every HR user who accesses candidate data through Axis Strive

### 7. Contractor/Intern IP Assignment & NDA (Reusable Template)

**Between:** Strive Pulse and any contractor, freelancer, or intern
**Signed by:** Yash + the individual
**Use this:** Before anyone outside the three founders touches code, design, or product — no exceptions, even for a weekend favor from a friend.

**What it covers:** Confirms it's an independent engagement (not employment), assigns all work product to the company, confidentiality obligations that survive the engagement, no-conflicting-obligations representation.

## Part 2: Private Limited Company Registration

Registration is done through the MCA's integrated **SPICe+ (INC-32)** form, which bundles name reservation, incorporation, PAN, TAN, and GST registration into a single filing.

### Documents Required

**A. Personal documents (× 3, one set per founder)**

| # | Document | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | PAN card | Already have |
| 2 | Aadhaar card | Already have |
| 3 | Secondary ID (Passport/Voter ID/DL) | Already have |
| 4 | Address proof — bank/electricity/phone bill, **under 2 months old** | Get a fresh one |
| 5 | Passport-size photograph | Quick to get |
| 6 | **Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)**, Class 3 | **Must newly obtain** — see below |

**B. Registered office proof**

| # | Document | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 7 | Utility bill, under 2 months old, matching the address | Whichever address you register |
| 8 | Rent agreement (if rented) or ownership/sale deed (if owned) | |
| 9 | NOC from the property owner | Mandatory even with a rent agreement |

**C. Company-level documents (drafted, not collected)**

| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 10 | e-MOA (Memorandum of Association) | Name, objects, liability, subscription clauses |
| 11 | e-AOA (Articles of Association) | Internal governance rules |
| 12 | INC-9 declaration | Auto-generated in SPICe+, signed with DSC |
| 13 | DIR-2 (consent to act as director) | Each of you signs |
| 14 | Two proposed company names | Check availability first (see below) |

**D. The step that needs a professional**

| # | Requirement | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 15 | **Practicing professional certification** | A CA, CS, CMA, or Advocate must digitally certify the SPICe+ filing — **not DIY-able** |
| 16 | MCA name search | Free — do this yourself first |
| 17 | IP India trademark search | Free — check "Strive Pulse" isn't already trademarked |
| 18 | AGILE-PRO-S (bundled in SPICe+) | Auto-handles GSTIN, EPFO, ESIC, bank account request |

### How to Get the DSC (the one genuinely new step)

1. Choose a Certifying Authority: eMudhra, Sify, or NSDL
2. Complete Aadhaar-based e-KYC (webcam photo + Aadhaar OTP) — done entirely online
3. Typically issued within a few hours
4. Cost: roughly ₹800–₹2,000 per director — all three of you need your own

### Suggested Order of Operations

1. **MCA name search + IP India trademark search** for "Strive Pulse" (free, do this first — everything else depends on it)
2. **Decide the registered office address** (a residential address is acceptable)
3. **Obtain DSC** for all three founders
4. **Engage a CA/CS in Surat** to handle the SPICe+ filing and professional certification
5. File **SPICe+** with MOA, AOA, DIR-2, and supporting documents
6. Receive **Certificate of Incorporation**, PAN, TAN, and GSTIN together

### Realistic Timeline

- **5–7 working days** once all documents are ready and accurate
- **7–14 working days** is a safer real-world estimate — most delays come from a mismatched address on a utility bill or a name rejection, not the core paperwork
